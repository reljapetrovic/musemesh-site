musemesh

every phone in the room becomes a speaker

no app. scan a QR code, tap once — you're part of the sound system.

HOW IT WORKS

> a performer hosts a room — a tiny server in their DAW or app

> everyone joins the venue's wifi — performer and phones on the same network

> the crowd scans a QR code. no app, no signup — the browser is the speaker

> every phone plays its own channel, in sync

> the room becomes the sound system

RUN A ROOM

> load musemesh-server in your DAW (VST3/AU)

> send it channels with musemesh-channel

> show the QR — the crowd does the rest

[ download — free ]

macOS VST3/AU · ~29 MB · unsigned build — see INSTALL.txt inside

ABOUT

musemesh is an experiment in shared performance: instead of one PA, the audience's phones become a distributed speaker orchestra. built by relja petrovic + ai.
